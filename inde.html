A recipe is a structured set of instructions listing ingredients and methods—such as boiling, frying, or baking—to prepare a specific dish

often reflecting cultural traditions. Key steps typically involve preparing components (chopping/seasoning), applying heat until tender, and seasoning to taste, as shown in examples like making fried rice, stir-fried vegetables, or Indian curries.

Simple Fried Rice Recipe: To make simple fried rice, first prepare your ingredients by chopping onions, chilies, and garlic, and preparing some precooked jasmine rice.

Heat a small amount of oil in a wok or pan over medium-high heat, add the chopped aromatics, and fry until fragrant. Add vegetables like carrots or cabbage, then incorporate the rice, breaking up any clumps.

Stir in soy sauce or seasonings to taste, cooking until everything is thoroughly heated and combined.
